supported formats: .step Files for Laser Cut & Bending Parts

Get your parts properly designed for bending is essential to achieve an excellent finish with fast delivery.

  • Files with single isolated parts, without notes or assemblies
  • Parts in real size, scale 1:1 in millimeters
  • All bends with radius
  • Inner folding radius equal or less to the sheet thickness value
  • No chamfers or roundings
  • All cutting geometries perpendicular to the sheet
